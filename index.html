Memory Card Game


































Rule And Regulation
 1. All the cards are shuffled and placed face down on a flat.
    2. The player flips over two cards. If the two cards, match, the player keeps them and takes another turn. if        the cards do not match, the player flips them back over, and it becomes the next player's turn.
   3. The game continues in this way with players taking turns flipping over two cards at a time until all            the cards have been matched. The player with the most matches at the end of the game is declared the   winner.
